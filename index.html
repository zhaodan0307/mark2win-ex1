Schedule Arrange System

ASP .NET

Online Website

In shops such as coffee shops and milk tea shops, most employees are part-time, which means that the number of hours worked per week varies for everyone. As a result, weekly schedules require the store manager to rearrange them by asking employees when they can come in. However, after we have this system, the store manager does not need to spend time to arrange the schedule any more. Instead, everyone needs to input his or her working time next week one week in advance, and the schedule can be automatically arranged by the system.
